Welcome to HonorCode

A Brand new platform for organizing service and providing tutoring in a simple and organized way!

Expanding on the original concept of HonorHelp, HonorCode provides a platform for any school Honor Society to use to organize their service and provide tutoring to anyone else in Concordia International School Shanghai.

Why HonorCode?

Hand Crafted Systematic Tutoring Service

Created entirely from the ground up, this website provides a new system to effectively connect students to tutors based on times the tutors are available. Thus increasing efficiency and communication.

Within the Community

This website was created by students in Concordia International School Shanghai, for students in Concordia International School Shanghai.

Peer to Peer

One on one or group tutoring offered by our very own capable members of NHS and SNHS.

Dozens of Subjects

A multitude of subjects including but not limited to: Calculus, Chemistry, Physics, Computer Science, Biology, and more are offered at HonorHelp.

Honorcode Tutoring Step-By-Step

Calendar Based Tutoring Selection

Using the open source FullCalendar, the tutoring base calendar system was built from the ground up utilizing Javascript, PHP, and mySQL.

Email Automated
